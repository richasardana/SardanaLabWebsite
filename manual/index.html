Lab manual and policies

Please visit the Sardana lab Sharepoint site for lab policies and protocols. This is where you will find: mission, information about lab policies and expectations, guidelines for working in the lab, and data management plans

Our goal is to work as a team to develop creative approaches to answer fundamental questions in cell biology. We are also committed to work together in ensuring that we understand and support diversity, equity and inclusion in science. This is a living document that aims to assist us in achieving these goals more effectively.

The manual is templated on and draws from the Wallace lab manual.
